Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat, cheese, and tomato sauce. It's a hearty and comforting meal that is perfect for family gatherings or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 4 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Drain excess fat.
  4. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, salt, and pepper.
  5. Spread a layer of marinara sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  6. Layer noodles, ricotta mixture, ground beef, marinara sauce, and mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers.
  7. Top with remaining marinara sauce and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
  8. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes until cheese is bubbly.
  9. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving.

Notes

You can add vegetables like spinach or mushrooms for extra flavor. Lasagna can be made ahead of time and stored in the refrigerator or freezer.

Tips

For a vegetarian version, substitute ground beef with sautéed vegetables or lentils. You can also use no-boil lasagna noodles to save time.

Serving Suggestions

Serve with garlic bread and a side salad for a complete meal. Pair with a glass of red wine for an authentic Italian experience.

Storage Instructions

Store leftovers in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Reheat in the oven or microwave before serving.

Nutrition Information

Approximate nutrition per serving (based on 8 servings): 350 calories, 20g protein, 25g carbohydrates, 15g fat.

Variations

Try different types of cheese like feta or goat cheese for a unique flavor. You can also experiment with different sauces, such as pesto or Alfredo.
